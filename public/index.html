Click here for the Git-Hub repository of the project. The protocols on the right are dynamically fetched from this endpoint. Try out the editor by creating new (button on the top right corner) trials, blocks or groups (parent-blocks) and dragging them to other instances. This will result in the child data being nested in the parent. With the preview buttons you can inspect each instance in JSON format. After editing the protocols, you can press the export button (top right) to see the output subjects.json.
