Click here for the Git-Hub repository of the project. Protocols can be dynamically fetched from this endpoint by pressing the "fetch" button. Try out the editor by creating new trials, blocks or groups (parent-blocks) and dragging them to other instances. This will result in the child data being copied into the parent. For now, I keep both flat (only trials) and nested data. With the "preview" buttons you can inspect each instance in JSON format. After editing the protocols, you can press the export button to see the output subjects.json. You can also reset everything with the "reset" button.
